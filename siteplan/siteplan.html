Pickleville

Site Name

I chose pickleville.com because it represents a community or "village" of people that have interests in pickleball.

Site Purpose

The site focuses on helping people learn about pickleball and/or to help find communities and courts to play pickleball.

Scenarios

  • Wheres a pickleball court near me?
  • How do I play?
  • When do others play?

Color Schema

  • Primary: Skyblue will be used for background colors (depending on the contrast ratio.)
  • Secondary: White will be used for heading elements and paragraph text and/or background colors (depending on the contrast ratio.)
  • Tertiary: Dark gray will be used for heading elements or borders.
  • Accent: Sunflowerlemon will be used for small elements like borders and navigation elements and hover effects.

Typography

I'm using Luckiest Guy for my heading font and Montserrat for my body font. Arial Black and Sans-Serif will be the default fallback font.
